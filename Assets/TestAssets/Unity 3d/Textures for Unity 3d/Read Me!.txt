this character contains three materials.

1) "akira body" - in this material you will have to apply the textures in the "body" folder. it contains albedo,metallic,normal, roughness maps.

2) "hair"       - in this material you will have to apply the hair texture which is situated in hair folder. you will have to set the shader to transparent.

3) "cornea/glass" - in this material you will have to make a glass shader in unity.
